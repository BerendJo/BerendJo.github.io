I offer a wide variety of services in the Media design field. If you don't exactly know what you want, don't hestitate to contact me. I would be more than happy to discuss the possibilites.

Webdesign

I will design the overall layout and aesthetic of your website, with interactive mockups to present a perfect view of how the website will look. This is always discussed thoroughly with the client to ensure the product matches the client's view.

Web Development

I will code a responsive (Mobile, desktop) website using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (and more). The website can be fully customized to the client's needs.

Branding

I will setup things such as, but not limited to: the company logo, font and color scheme. To ensure the client has enough design options to choose from. If the client is unsatisfied I will design and create more until the client's needs are fulfilled.

UI/UX Design

I will design the layout of an app or website. I will ensure the user experience is optimized for the target audience.

Graphics Design

Consultancy
